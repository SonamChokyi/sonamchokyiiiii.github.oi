Tibetan Sky Burial

Personal views

1.Sky burial is considered by Tibetans as a kind of almsgiving. If you think sky burial is very cruel, it may be because you are too attached to the physical body of this life.

2.It is not good to make value judgments about this practice without your deep understanding.

3.I think sky burial is also a culture that deserves to be respected.

Tips to visit Tibet

1.Your presence at a sky burial won’t be a welcome one, especially if you are holding a camera.

2.During the ritual rites, your presence is extremely offensive for the local residences because you have disturbed the peace of the dead people.

3.The lamas will ask you to leave with intense anger and hate.
